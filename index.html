About Me

Hello,

My name is Ludmila Lemashov I am 81 years old, I was born in Almaty Kazakhstan in 1939. I worked as a water engineer in Tashket, Uzbekistan I was married to Michael Lemashov 1935-2011. Michael worked as a mechanical engineer. My father served as a pilot in the soviet union army, and my mother was an accountant. I have two brothers Genady and Alexander, Genady is an Architect and Alexander was a Dentist. Because of my father being pilot and officer my family often moved from country to country all over the soviet union for example: kazakhstan, russia, uzbekistan, georgia. I came to israel in 1994, I live in the city Ashdod i have 5 grandsons and 1 great grandchild.
